About Me

I'm an experienced iOS developer based in Nepal with a strong track record in the software industry. Currently driving mobile development at KMG Group, one of Nepal's leading digital media companies.

Alongside my professional work, I'm pursuing a Master's degree in Computer Engineering at Nepal College of Information Technology (Pokhara University), continually deepening my foundations in software systems and engineering.
